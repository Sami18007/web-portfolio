Online Pizza Store - Front-End

Create your own pizza and make an order. This practise project consists of front-end and back-end. Orders can be made either after or without logging in, and in the former case users can view their ordering history. Users can modify their information after the sign up process also.
React, TypeScript, Axios, InkScape

Online Pizza Store - Back-End

This is the back-end for the online pizza store. It is connected to a database where users and orders are stored. Certain APIs need the user to be authenticated, which is taken care of with a token. Available paths: GET "/", POST "/login", POST "/users", GET "/users/:username", PUT "/users/:username", GET "/users/:username/orders" and POST "/orders".
NodeJS, Express, JavaScript, Mongoose, Bcrypt, JSONWebToken, dotenv
